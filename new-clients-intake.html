new clients

intake forms

After formally scheduling your initial consultation with a provider at Union Square Practice, you will receive a confirmation email that includes an intake document. If you have trouble accessing that document, or did not receive the intake form via email, please download and complete the appropriate forms below. Once you have completed the forms, you can bring the completed hard copy documents to your first appointment with your provider. If you would like to send your forms electronically, please send an email to new@unionsquarepractice.com that includes the completed forms and the name of the provider you will be seeing.

There will also be additional hard copies of all necessary forms at our office.

If you have any questions about the forms, please contact us.
